              Instructions

              You are presented with a board of squares. Some squares contain mines (bombs), others don't. If you click on a square containing a mine you lose. You win by "clearing" all the squares that do not contain a mine.

              The first clicked or touched square is never a mine.

              After you win or lose a game, click or touch the yellow emoji face to start a new game.

              Game Controls

              Left clicking or touching a square that doesn't have a mine "clears" the square and reveals the number of adjacent squares* containing mines. If the square has no adjacent mines, that square will be blank and all blank squares radiating outward will be cleared until a square with an adjacent mine is revealed. Use this information to avoid the mines.

              You can flag squares you think are mines. To flag a square that you think is a mine right click on it. To remove the flag right click the square again. If you flag a mine incorrectly you will have to remove the flag and clear the square before you can win. You don't have to flag all the mines to win. The flags are just a tool to help you remember where you think there is a mine. To win you still need to clear all squares without mines.

              On smaller screens the game board and Leaderboard scroll left and right.

              *Adjacent squares are those squares directly above, below, left, right, and at each corner of the clicked square.

              Game Scoreboard Information

              At the start of the game the upper left corner indicates the number of hidden mines. When you flag a square this number decreases by one (and increases by one if a flag is removed). You cannot flag more squares than the number of mines on the board.

              The upper right corner is a timer in seconds. Try to clear the mines as fast as possible. If the timer reaches 999 before all the mines are cleared the game ends.

              Leaderboard and Your Best Scores

              The Leaderboard contains the top 10 fastest times of all users with an account for each level. If you have an account and are logged in you will also see your ten best scores for each level under Your Best Scores.

              Create Your Own Game Board

              Select the number of squares you want for the width and height, and the number of mines to hide. Then click the Customize button. Your choices determines the difficulty of clearing the board.
